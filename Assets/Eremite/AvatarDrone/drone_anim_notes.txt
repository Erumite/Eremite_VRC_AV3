Using only a single parameter to drive as much behavior as possible.

Syncing is not needed as the majority of the animations are played locally.


INT - DroneToggles
 - Enable/Disable/Reset
   * 1 = Enable Drone DT
   * 2 = Disable Drone
   * 3 = Drone Return
   * 4 = Enable Drone VR

 - Toggle Addons:
   * 5 = All OFF
   * 6 = Light Toggle
